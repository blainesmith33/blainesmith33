# Blaine Smith

Builder of systems for human capability, governance, knowledge continuity, and AI-augmented execution.

## Mission

Helping people see clearly enough to exercise agency.

My work focuses on transforming observations into knowledge, knowledge into decisions, and decisions into meaningful action.

Through governance systems, knowledge infrastructure, and human-centered AI, I am exploring how transparency, honesty, and continuity can strengthen human capability rather than diminish it.

---

## Ecosystem

### The Human Capability Project (THCP)

An open initiative focused on human capability, agency, governance, and human-centered AI.

### Knowledge Governance Infrastructure (KGI)

Systems for preserving, governing, validating, and operationalizing knowledge generated through human and AI collaboration.

### Personal Engineering Intelligence System (PEIS)

Operational planning, execution, evaluation, decision tracking, and continuity systems for complex work.

### UMB Governance Stack

Foundational governance, validation, and infrastructure concepts that evolved into the broader ecosystem.
